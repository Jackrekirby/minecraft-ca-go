Game Controls

  • : Unlock Controls
  • ESC: Lock Controls
  • ?: Toggle Menu
  • Mouse: Rotate Camera
  • Left Click: Place/Interact Block
  • Right Click: Destroy Block
  • Middle Click: Pick Block
  • Q: Quit the game
  • P: Toggle Play/Pause
  • O: Step One Iteration
  • R: Reset the world
  • W: Move forward
  • A: Move left
  • S: Move backward
  • D: Move right
  • E: Move up
  • C: Move down
  • Z: Rotate left
  • X: Rotate right
